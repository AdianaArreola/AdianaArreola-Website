My teaching skills work in many environments. I have tutored students in mathematics throughout grades three through twelve. When working with a new person I take time to ask what the student is struggling with and is successful with. After discovering where the student is struggling I have them solve problems they know how to solve. When solving those problems I have them account aloud how they are solving the problem. By discovering how the student solves the problems the best, I use their steps to teach them how to solve the problems they are struggling with. By using this I am able to help students overcome the subjects they are struggling in.
